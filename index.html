About Me

Dr. A. S. Reyas is a first-generation entrepreneur known for his clarity of vision, risk-taking nature, and strong sense of social responsibility. His entrepreneurial journey is rooted in the Real Estate and Interior Designing industry, where he gained practical business experience and delivered several exemplary residential and commercial projects.

He added vibrancy to the Madurai cityscape through his enterprise, AR Residency Builders & Promoters. His creative mindset and dynamic execution later led him to leadership roles in manufacturing and business development.
